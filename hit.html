Create an Article Outline about New York City

The Carnegie Mellon Social Computing Laboratory is conducting research to study how people write articles on the internet. Imagine that you are planning to write an article about New York City. Please create an outline for this article in the form below.

Rules

  • Please write one topic per line in the order that you think it should appear in the article.
  • Please provide 4 to 8 outline headings.

Example

For example, an article on Lions might have the following outline:

  1. Biological classification
  2. Physical characteristics
  3. Biology and behavior
  4. Distribution and habitat
  5. Population and conservation status
  6. Cultural depictions
